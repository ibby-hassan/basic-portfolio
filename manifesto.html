Manifesto

Salaam alaikum wa rahmatullah.

SubhanAllah, another term’s come and gone. This will be my final university year, and fourth involved with GUMSA. That's four years spent learning from, laughing with, and growing alongside this community.
Maryam and Yusuf are strong candidates, each capable of bringing their own unique strengths to the role. I am grateful to have been given the opportunity to run alongside them.
Between them, my pledges are in some way similar. I look to expand student activism, awareness, and the muslim voice.
But, most uniquely, I aim to break the clique culture within GUMSA.

Alhamdulillah, I have been given a broad network, know-how, and an intimate perspective on the university landscape from many roles within it. Year after year, I’ve watched committees build on each other. Presently, I forecast many new, impressionable faces joining us.
Now more than ever, the community needs a voice and platform where the individual voice is heard, and the collective voice is unified.

In action, I pledge to keep the momentum going - maintain our classes, maintain our outreach. But to also create regular feedback forms, enlist general members on projects such as Charity Week, or newsletters, and generally serve, rather than govern.
In this way I hope to acclimatise our new members to our community, as well as strengthen the platform for those to come.

May Allah SWT keep our intentions pure, hearts soft, and resolve firm. “Verily, Allah is All-Knowing, All-Wise."

Ameen.
