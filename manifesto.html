Manifesto

Salaam alaikum wa rahmatullah.

SubhanAllah, another term’s come and gone. This will be my final university year, and fourth involved with GUMSA. That's four years spent learning from, laughing with, and growing alongside this community.
Maryam and Yusuf are strong candidates, each capable of bringing their own unique strengths to the role. I am grateful to have been given the opportunity to run alongside them.
Indeed, from amongst any of you, I consistently find reasons to smile, reminders of purpose, and humility at what we can achieve together.
Through all that, there is just one thing I uniquely offer: my experiences, and all that comes with it.

Alhamdulillah, I have been given a broad network, know-how, and an intimate perspective on the university landscape from many roles within it. Year after year, I’ve watched committees build on each other. Presently, I forecast many new, impressionable faces joining us. However, it is also an unprecedented, and unfortunately divisive time for the ummah.
Thus, I feel GUMSA presidency is where I’m best placed to provide a space of unity, community, and security for education and religious expression.

In action, I pledge to keep the momentum going - maintain an Islamic outreach, expand essential dialogues and education both Islamic and societal, involve general members with committee such as through Charity Week planning, refine committee workflows, etc.
In this way I hope to acclimatise our new members to our community, as well as strengthen the platform for those to come.
This also aims to combat clique culture.

May Allah SWT keep our intentions pure, hearts soft, and resolve firm. “Verily, Allah is All-Knowing, All-Wise."

Ameen.
