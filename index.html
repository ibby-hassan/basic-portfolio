IBRAHIM FOR GUMSA PRESIDENT

Manifesto

Salaam alaikum wa rahma.

SubhanAllah, another term’s come and gone. This will be my final university year, and fourth involved with GUMSA. That's four years spent learning from, laughing with, and growing alongside this community.
Though unsure whom else is running for the role, I am sure of their integrity and their worth; from amongst you I consistently find reasons to smile, reminders of purpose, and humility at what we can achieve together.
Between the strong, the brave, the intellectual, the old and the new, there is just one thing I uniquely offer: experience, and all that comes with it.

Alhamdulillah, I have been given a broad network, know-how, and an intimate perspective on the university landscape from many roles within it. Year after year, I’ve watched committees build on each other. Presently, I forecast many new, impressionable faces joining us. However, it is also an unprecedented, divisive time for the ummah.
Thus, I feel GUMSA presidency is where I’m best placed to provide a space of unity, community, and security for education and religious expression.

In action, I pledge to keep the momentum going - maintain an Islamic presence, expand dialogue and education, involve general members with committee events such as Charity Week planning, refine committee workflows, etc.
In this way I hope to acclimatise our new members to our community, as well as strengthen the platform for those to come.

May Allah SWT keep our intentions pure, hearts soft, and resolve firm. “Verily, Allah is All-Knowing, All-Wise."

Ameen.
